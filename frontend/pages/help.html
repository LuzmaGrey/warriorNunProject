How Can I Help?

Petition

Join the movement! Your signature can make a difference. If you haven't already, sign our petition now and be a part of creating positive change. But we don't stop there! Help us spread the word by sharing it on all your social media channels and reaching out to friends, family, and anyone else who can make an impact. Let's make our voices heard together!

Donate on GoFundMe

Be a part of a cause greater than yourself! Our Warrior Nun renewal projects needs your support to bring hope and inspiration to those in need. Your donation, no matter how big or small, will make a difference in the lives of many. Join us on GoFundMe today and let's make this project a reality.

Social Media

Let's make a difference together! We're fighting to #SaveWarriorNun and your support can help turn the tide. Join us on social media and show your support by using the hashtag #SaveWarriorNun. Let's make this cause go viral and save this beloved show!

Other Ways to Help

Join the #SaveWarriorNun campaign! Let Netflix know how much you love this show by sending emails or making phone calls. Give it a high rating on Rotten Tomatoes and stream both seasons on Netflix. Get creative and take personal initiative to spread the word and show your support!

Petition

Take action for representation now! Too many queer WLW-led shows have been denied a chance to thrive because of a lack of promotion and support from streaming platforms. Let's come together and make our voices heard. Sign the petition on Change.org and help us put pressure on these platforms to commit to diverse and inclusive stories. Join the #SaveWarriorNun movement and let's show that representation matters!

GoFundMe

Join the movement and make a difference! We are launching a series of powerful campaigns to bring attention to our cause and show streaming platforms and the media that we are dedicated and persistent in our fight for representation. Your support is critical to our success. Donate any amount on our GoFundMe page and help us reach our goal. Every penny will go towards our mission and your contribution can make all the difference. Be a part of the change and #SaveWarriorNun.

The do's and don'ts of using the hashtag

#SaveWarriorNun on social media

Carefully review this guide to use the hashtag correctly in order to increase the number of tweets. Please like and share with others

DON'T

Never use a hashtag without text and images. (ALWAYS check that it is spelled correctly).

#SaveWarrionNun

— Sister Warrior (@SamurayNun666) January 26, 2023

DON'T

Do NOT use only numbers, it will become spam. Please always add an image, gif or video

13 #SaveWarriorNun

— Sister Warrior (@SamurayNun666) January 26, 2023

DO

You can add an image and a small text next to the hashtag #SaveWarriorNun.

I need them together again! #SaveWarriorNun pic.twitter.com/N4g1YbpLE0

— Sister Warrior (@SamurayNun666) January 26, 2023

DO

Add a short coherent text and add the hashtag #SaveWarriorNun

Pain Is What Made me a Sister Warrior. #SaveWarriorNun

— Sister Warrior (@SamurayNun666) January 26, 2023
